Satisfactory

What is Satisfactory?

Satisfactory is a factory game developed by Coffee Stain Studios that was fully
released on September 10th of 2024. In the game you are a pioneer that goes around
the planet and collect resources to build massive factories to produce parts for
your corperation, Ficsit, in order to 'save the day'


What things do you do in game?

  • Build huge factories around the map
  • Collect hard drives so unlock recipes
  • Complete milestones through the space elevator
  • Explore and find alien life and artifacts
