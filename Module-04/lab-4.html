Good Meal Prep Ideas

Introduction

Spending some time on your weekends to prepare meals for the week ahead is a great idea. I allows you to save money instead of ordering out, and it also allows you to try new foods!

Meal Ideas

Food Details
Pasta w/ Meat Sauce This is an easy meal to cook, and you can make a lot of it for not a large cost. You can also mix it up and try different sauces and meats.
Chicken Stir Fry Allows for many different flavors of stir fry, as well as being an easy way to add different veggies to your meals. You can also make rice to go with it.
Quesadillas Base quesadillas only need tortillas and cheese, so after that you can add anything you want. I personally like to make buffalo chicken quesadillas with some blue cheese inside.
